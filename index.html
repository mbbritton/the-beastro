The Concept

Fine dining. Reimagined for pet people.

📋

Step 1: The Questionnaire

When you arrive, you'll fill out a short (but very important) questionnaire about your pet. Their quirks, their obsessions, their most chaotic moments.

👨‍🍳

Step 2: The Chef Studies

Our chef reviews your answers with great seriousness and theatrical gravity. Pacing may occur. Muttering is expected.

🍽️

Step 3: The Creation

A dish or drink arrives at your table — crafted to capture the essence of your pet. Whimsical. Delicious. Inexplicably accurate.

🐾

The Fine Print

No two dishes are ever the same. Your pet is unique. Your plate should be too. Pets are welcome in spirit only — health codes remain, unfortunately, non-negotiable.

The Menu

There is no menu.

Everything here is made to order — made for your pet. But here's how it works:

🥂

Cocktails & Mocktails

Flavor profiles drawn from your pet's personality. Chaotic pets get complex layers. Calm pets get something clean and elegant. Cats who steal Twizzlers get something red and unapologetically sweet.

🧀

Small Plates & Boards

Charcuterie, tasting plates, and shareable bites — arranged and themed after your pet's vibe. Expect surprising garnishes. Expect the chef to take it very seriously.

🍮

Sweets & Desserts

For the sweet-natured. For the ones who nap in warm spots. For the ones who are, objectively, someone's little baby.

🍳

Mains

Full plates for the most complex pets. Usually reserved for dogs who do the sploot, cats with multiple documented nicknames, or any animal that has inspired genuine concern in a veterinarian.

🐾 Pricing: Your creation is priced based on ingredient complexity and how long the chef stares out the window thinking about your pet. Typically $14–$32.

🐾 Dietary restrictions: Please note yours in the questionnaire. The chef will accommodate. The chef will not, however, be making anything for the pets themselves. They asked.

🐾 Reservations: Required. Use the questionnaire above. Walk-ins are accepted only if your pet's story is sufficiently compelling.
